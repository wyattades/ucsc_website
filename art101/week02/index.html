How to Make a Flavorful Burger:

(If you don't have any of the following ingredients, substitute something similar)

1. Mold ground beef into the shape of a patty and make sure it's about a centimeter thick
2. Rub in a generous amount of salt and pepper on both sides
3. Heat a frying pan to medium-high and add a light layer of vegetable oil (or use a grill if you own one!)
4. Fry the patty. After about a minute on each side, wipe some yellow mustard on both sides. Continue frying (and flipping occasionaly) until patty is medium well
5. Toast a brioche bun, or whatever bread you have
6. Spread mayonnaise and ketchup on one of the buns
7. Combine buns, fresh spinach, patty, and some sauteed mushrooms for a delicious home-made burger!
